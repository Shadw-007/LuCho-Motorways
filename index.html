Your Comfort and safety are of utmost Importance and value

At LuCho Motorways, we prioritize the comfort and safety of our valued customers when they travel with us. We understand that transportation is not merely about reaching a destination but also about the experience and peace of mind throughout the journey. That's why we have implemented various measures to ensure that every passenger feels secure and at ease when they choose our transportation services.

First and foremost, we have invested heavily in maintaining a modern and well-maintained fleet of vehicles. Our coaches undergo regular inspections, servicing, and maintenance to ensure they are in excellent working condition. We have strict quality control procedures in place to address any potential issues promptly. By upholding these standards, we strive to provide our customers with reliable transportation options.

The safety of our passengers is of paramount importance to us. To achieve this, we have implemented comprehensive safety protocols and practices. Our drivers and staff members undergo rigorous training programs to ensure they are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to handle various situations. We strictly adhere to traffic regulations and prioritize responsible driving practices. Additionally, all our vehicles are equipped with state-of-the-art safety features, including seat belts, airbags, and advanced braking systems.

At LuCho Motorways, we consider our passengers as our utmost priority. We are committed to providing them with a comfortable, safe, and enjoyable travel experience. By upholding the highest standards of quality, safety, and customer service, we aim to exceed the expectations of our customers and build long-lasting relationships based on trust and satisfaction.

D e s t i n a t i o n s

Livingstone

Livingstone City, named after the famous explorer David Livingstone, is a vibrant town in southern Zambia, serving as a gateway to the stunning Victoria Falls and offering a mix of historical charm and natural beauty.

Lusaka

Lusaka, the capital and largest city of Zambia, is a bustling urban center with a mix of modern amenities, government institutions, and cultural attractions, making it the political and economic hub of the country.

Ndola

Ndola, Zambia's third-largest city, is an important industrial and commercial hub in the Copperbelt region, known for its copper mining activities and vibrant economic development.

Kitwe

Kitwe, a major city in the Copperbelt province of Zambia, is renowned for its significant role in the country's copper mining industry and serves as an essential economic and commercial center in the region.

Chingola

Chingola, situated in Zambia's Copperbelt province, is recognized for its copper mining activities and is a key player in the country's mining sector, contributing to both economic development and employment opportunities

Solwezi

Solwezi, the provincial capital of North-Western Province in Zambia, is an emerging town with growing economic importance, particularly due to its role in the mining industry and as a center for commerce and administration in the region.
